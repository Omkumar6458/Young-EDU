What is the Fibonacci Series?

The Fibonacci series is a sequence of numbers where each number is the sum of the two preceding ones, starting from 0 and 1. The sequence goes like this: 0, 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, and so on.

Example

How it Works

Each term in the series is calculated as:

  • F(n) = F(n-1) + F(n-2)
  • Where F(0) = 0 and F(1) = 1
