What is LCM?

The Least Common Multiple (LCM) of two numbers is the smallest number that is a multiple of both numbers. It is used in various mathematical operations and real-life situations like finding the least common time to meet, synchronizing events, etc.

How to Calculate LCM?

The LCM of two numbers can be calculated using several methods. Here's one approach:

  • Find the multiples of each number.
  • List the common multiples.
  • The smallest common multiple is the LCM.

Example

Let's find the LCM of 12 and 15:

  • Multiples of 12: 12, 24, 36, 48, 60, ...
  • Multiples of 15: 15, 30, 45, 60, 75, ...
  • Common multiples: 60, ...
  • LCM of 12 and 15 is 60.
