What Are Even and Odd Numbers?

An even number is any number that can be divided by 2 without leaving a remainder. Examples: 2, 4, 6, 8.

An odd number is any number that cannot be divided by 2 without leaving a remainder. Examples: 1, 3, 5, 7.
